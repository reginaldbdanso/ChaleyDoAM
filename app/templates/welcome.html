Welcome to ChaleyDoAm

A task management app to help you stay organized and productive.

Features Get Started About

Edit Feature

Made a mistake? Don't stress. You can easily edit your tasks just by clicking this button.

Delete Feature

Maybe you've changed your mind and no longer want to add that particular task to your list. This button will come in handy.

Export Feature

Take your tasks with you wherever you go and on which ever device you want. This feature produces a calendar file. This file is useful for sharing your tasks with others or for importing your tasks into another calendar app.

About

ChaleyDoAm is a task management app that helps you stay organized and productive. It is a simple and easy to use app that allows you to create tasks and manage them.

ChaleyDoAm is a project created by a group of students from the ALX SE program. It was created as part of a Software Engineering course.

ChaleyDoAm is a Pidgin word that means "Bro do it".

ChaleyDoAm is a free app that is available to anyone who wants to use it.

ChaleyDoAm is a web app that can be accessed from any device that has an internet connection.
